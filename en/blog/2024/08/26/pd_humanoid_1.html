1954-05-03、ポルトガル： UFO/ET の目撃

August 26, 2024 · 15 min read

Preston Dennett による要約​

(case1)
ETと円盤 1954年5月5日の午後、8歳のアルナルド・リベイロ・デ・フレイタスは、ポルトガルのフレギムにある自宅近くでヤギの世話をしていた。1機は飛び去ったが、もう1機は残り、アルナルドにはその中に2体の大きな頭のヒューマノイドが座っているのが見えた。そして、そのうちの1体が彼を正視していた！飛び去った後、それは地面に奇妙な着陸の痕跡を残した。

今日お話ししたい最初のケースは、「ETと円盤」である。 これは目撃者一人の事件だが、明らかに着陸の痕跡がある。 この事件は1954年5月5日にポルトガルのフレギムで起こった。 この事件は研究者ビセンテ・フアン・バリスター・オルモスによるものである。 (00:01:56)

1954年5月5日の午後2時、8歳のアルナルド・リベイロ・デ・フリティスは、彼が住んでいたポルトガルのフレギム・アマランテという小さな村を囲む丘で、ヤギの世話をしていた。 当時のこの地域は今よりもずっと田舎で、松の木が生い茂る荒野が広がっていた。 突然、彼はヤギを放牧していた。その時、彼は奇妙な音を耳にした。口笛のようなもので、ラジオの静止音と干渉音に似ていると彼は言った。 (00:02:32)

それは彼から500フィートほど離れた地形のくぼみから聞こえているようだった。 そこで彼は調査するためにそこに向かって走った。 10秒後、彼はその場所にたどり着き、音が近くの玉子川に向かって飛び去る物体から聞こえているのを見た。 彼はその物体が地面から上方に上昇し、ゆっくりと自転するのを見た。 その物体は60フィートか70フィートの高さに達し、北に向かって猛スピードで加速した。 (00:03:05)

しかしその一方で、アルナルドは2つ目の物体に気づいた。それは地形のくぼみの真ん中にあり、わずか130フィートほどしか離れていなかった。 それほど大きくはなかった。 高さは4フィート、幅は6フィートほどで、半球のような形をしていた。 グレーのメタリックな円形の土台が底を囲んでいて、上のドームは透明で、ドーム自体には茶色の砲塔があった。 (00:03:35)

この船の中に、彼は普通には見えない二人の人型を見ることができた。 (00:03:41)

二人とも頭がとても大きく、目が大きく、間隔が広く、口が開いていて、頭には触角のようなものがあった。 二人とも鎧を思わせる鮮やかな青い制服を着ていた。 一人はレバーのようなものを操作しているように見えたが、もう一人は目撃者をじっと見つめていた。 彼には二人とも座っているように見えたという。 (00:04:07)

そして彼は、このようなものを間近で見るには長い時間である約20秒間、絶対的な驚きの中で彼らを見ていた。 そして何の前触れもなく、この物体は飛び立ち、消えてしまった。

(00:04:34)

そして少なくともその後20年間はその状態が続くことになる。 そこには何も生えていなかった。 ほとんどの点で、私はそれをヒューマノイドとの典型的なUFO遭遇と呼ぶだろう。 私が気に入っているのは、目撃者がETを間近で見たことだ。 また、彼は明らかにこのテーマについて多くの知識を持っていなかったので、作り話である可能性は低い。 (00:05:02)

また、明らかに着陸の痕跡があったという事実を考慮すれば、その証言にさらなる信憑性を与えることになる。

And the first case I'd like to talk about today I call the ETs and the Saucer. This is a single witness case, though it does involve apparent landing traces. It occurred on May 5, 1954 in Fregim, Portugal, and it's a pretty interesting case. This case comes from researcher Vicente Juan Balister Olmos. (00:01:56)

It was 2pm on the afternoon of May 5, 1954 as 8-year-old Arnaldo Ribeiro de Fritis tended to some of his goats in the hills surrounding the small village of Fregim Amarante, Portugal, where he lived. This area back then was far more rural than it is today, lots of wilderness, thick with pine trees. And suddenly he's out there herding his goats when he hears this strange sound, kind of like a whistling, that he said resembled a radio receiving static and interference. (00:02:32)

And it seemed to be coming from a dip in the terrain about 500 feet away from him. So he ran towards it to investigate. Ten seconds later he reached this area and saw that the sound was coming from an object which was now flying off towards the nearby Tamago River. He watched it rise upwards from the ground, slowly revolving on its axis. It then reached a height of about 60 or 70 feet and accelerated at high speed towards the north, at which point it did emit a powerful hot wind. (00:03:05)

But meanwhile, Arnaldo noticed a second object which was sitting in the middle of this dip in the terrain, only about 130 feet away. And it wasn't that large. It was about 4 feet tall, maybe 6 feet wide, shaped like a half sphere. He said it had a grey metallic circular base which went around the bottom of it, but the dome on top was transparent and the dome itself was topped by a brown colored turret. (00:03:35)

Inside this craft he could see two humanoid figures which didn't look quite normal. (00:03:41)

They both had very large heads, large, widely spaced eyes, just an opening for a mouth, and what looked like antennae on their heads. They both wore bright blue uniforms that reminded Arnaldo of armor. One of the beings appeared to be operating a lever type device while the other was staring directly at the witness. He says it looked to him like they were both sitting down. (00:04:07)

And he watched them in absolute surprise for about 20 seconds, which is a long time when you're seeing something like this so close up. Then, without warning, this object took off and it was gone. Now later, checking out this area, he discovered that the location where the second craft had risen from the ground was barren of growth in an area of about 10 by 25 feet. (00:04:34)

And it would remain that way for at least the next 20 years. Nothing would grow there. In most ways, I would call that your typical UFO encounter with humanoids. What I like about it is the witness did get a very close up look at these ETs. And also, he clearly didn't have a whole lot of knowledge about this subject, so it's not likely that he made it up. (00:05:02)

And also, considering the fact that there were apparent landing traces, that always lends an extra level of credibility to the account.

▼展開 Few experiences are as astounding as a direct face-to-face encounter with an extraterrestrial humanoid from another world. And yet, the number of cases is far greater than most people think. They have been reported for well over a hundred years and probably into antiquity. Cases come from all over the world. This video presents ten little known cases, each one adding to our knowledge of ET contact. There are far too many cases to be explained away as hoaxes, hallucinations, or misperceptions.

THE ETS AND THE SAUCER. On the afternoon of May 5, 1954, eight-year-old Arnaldo Ribeiro de Freitas was goat-herding near his home in Fregim, Portugal when an odd sound alerted him to two UFOs hovering nearby. One flew off, but the other remained, and Arnaldo could see two big-headed humanoids sitting inside it. And one was looking right at him! After it departed, it left weird landing traces on the ground.

ANOTHER ALIEN MICHELIN MAN. One afternoon in May of 1960, Professor Miguel Timermans Ceballos was driving his motorcycle near Cadiz, Spain. Looking ahead, he saw a strange humanoid figure which looked very much like the famous Michelin Man. Seconds later, a second figure appeared. Both walked clumsily across the road before disappearing into the brush. Miguel was convinced they were not human.

IT WASN’T ANYTHING HUMAN. Late in the evening of October 23, 1963, Robert W. Smith drove in a rural area of South River, NJ when he saw something odd in the beams of his car headlights: three very short humanoid figures dressed in tight-fitting, silver, one-piece suits. They darted across the road far faster than a human could move. Robert was too frightened to stop. It was an experience he would never forget.

THE FLOATING HUMANOID OF COFICO. On July 2, 1968, numerous residents of Cofico, Argentina observed UFOs and/or humanoids hovering overhead. Three witnesses at one home described a glowing humanoid figure floating in the air. Another witness described a 9-foot-tall robotic figure, also floating in the sky. Another witness in a different home described the same thing. Even policemen witnessed strange activity.

THE JAY-WALKING ALIENS. In early May of 1969, numerous residents in Rockville, Virginia started seeing UFOs darting around overhead. But on the night of May 11, 1969, Mike Luczkowich saw actual humanoids. Driving along Route 622, he saw what he first thought were two deer. Instead, he realized they were four-foot-tall humanoids with giant heads. They crossed the road in front of him, followed by a third one.

THE DUMBBELL ETS. On the night of October 24, 1976, Romeo Lucarelli and his young daughter were driving to their home in Hobling, France when a brilliant light appeared ahead of them. It zoomed towards them and revealed itself to be a dumbbell-shaped object with two humanoid figures inside. To Romeo’s fear, the craft followed them home. His wife ran out just in time to see the UFO dart away.

IT DIDN’T SEEM LIKE A NORMAL PERSON. On the night of January 21, 1977, Juan Pedro Hernandez of Camuy, Puerto Rico was shocked to see an ET craft hovering at low altitude overhead. Inside he saw a skinny figure with a big head and dark eyes looking at him. Suddenly he was struck a beam of light from the UFO. The beam retracted and the UFO departed. Juan followed, but govt agents appeared, blocking his way.

THE UFO GNOME. In late March of 1978, Constantina Imbibos and her husband began seeing UFOs behind their home in Villamare Di Sapri, Italy. After several sightings, on April 25,1978, Constantina saw another UFO, and seconds later, a strange gnome-like humanoid wearing an orange suit and pointed hat. It bounced around like a gnome. Soon more UFOs would be seen, and more gnome-like entities.

AN 8-FOOT-TALL GRAY. On the night of July 31, 2018, a military veteran with a Top Secret clearance was driving on State Route 13 just north of Mount Vernon, Ohio when an eight-foot-tall, incredibly thin, hairless humanoid crossed the road in front of him. It had black eyes, a small mouth and four fingers on each hand. It was not human. Truly frightened, the witness decided not to stop.

I SAW SOMETHING I WILL NEVER FORGET. Around 5am on July 16, 2020, Paul Froggatt was bicycling home from work in Warwick, UK when a UFO appeared. He snapped two photos with his camera, but became frightened when the craft seemed to follow him. Moments later he came face-to-face with a 7-foot-tall, praying mantis humanoid. He was so frightened he began to cry. He fled the area, thoroughly traumatized.

Thankfully, as the government UFO coverup collapses, and as more people reveal their encounters, the truth is coming out. ETs are real! And it’s time that all of humanity knows it.
