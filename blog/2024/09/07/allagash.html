  1. アラガッシュ・ウォーターウェイ・アブダクション

アラガッシュ・ウォーターウェイ・アブダクションは、これまで記録された中で最も人気のあるグループ・アブダクション事件の一つと考えられている。

1976年8月20日、双子のジム・ワイナーとジャック・ワイナーは、二人の友人チャック・ラックとチャーリー・フォルツとともに、メイン州北部のアラガッシュ川沿いでのんびりと荒野を楽しむことにした。若い4人の美大生は高校時代からの友人で、ちょっとした裏山での冒険を楽しむことにしたのだ。

それから数日間、4人は水路に沿ってカヌーを漕いだりキャンプをしたりして、8月26日にイーグル湖に到着した。彼らはキャンプを張り、夜釣りでマスを釣ることにした。キャンプを出発する前に、彼らは必ず大きな焚き火を焚き、2～3時間燃え続ける大きな丸太を燃料にした。釣りを始めてしばらくして、チャックは自分たちが見張られているような気がすると言った。

振り向いたとき、大きな明るい球体が水面から2～3メートル上空を静かにホバリングしながら近づいてくるのが見えた。彼は他の人にそれを見るように叫び、4人全員が明るい色の球体を見た。

The Allagash Waterway Abduction is considered one of the most popular group abduction cases ever recorded. On August 20, 1976, twins Jim and Jack Weiner along with their two friends Chuck Rake and Charles Foltz decided to have a relaxing wilderness getaway along the Allagash River in northern Maine. The four young art students had been friends since high school and were excited for their backwoods adventure. (00:10:57)

For the next few days, the four canoed and camped along the waterway before reaching Eagle Lake on August 26. They set up camp and decided to go night fishing for some trout. Before heading out, they made sure to create a huge bonfire, fueling it with big logs that would burn for two to three hours at least, so they could easily find their way back. (00:11:19)

A short while after they started fishing, Chuck said he felt as if they were being watched. When he turned around, that's when he saw a large bright sphere silently hovering about two to three hundred feet above the water and coming towards them. He yelled at the others to look and all four saw the bright colored orb. Charlie took a flashlight and blinked at the object and that's when it stopped moving completely. (00:11:43)

At the same time, a bright tube of light hit the water. Both the object and a trail of beaming light suddenly began moving faster towards the canoe and the boys. They frantically paddled to get away, heading to their campsite, but shortly after, the beam completely engulfed them. What they remembered next differed for each person. Charlie remembers paddling for the shore and standing at the campsite, while Chuck remembers staying in the canoe after they reached the shore and staring at the bright glowing orb moving away. (00:12:14)

The twins remembered a bit more. They recalled paddling furiously, but realizing they couldn't get away from it. They were then standing on the shore and looking at the bright sphere hovering at twenty to thirty feet above the water. It stayed motionless for about four to five minutes before the beam pointed to the sky, it started to move, and then shot out of sight. (00:12:34)

After the shock wore off, Chuck moved out of the canoe to find the others and himself staring at the bonfire, which should have been burning bright. (00:12:42)

The whole encounter seemed like it happened in fifteen to twenty minutes, but the bonfire was completely burned out with nothing but red coals left. The boys would go on with their lives for years without ever trying to explore what really had happened. But Jim eventually suffered a head injury causing Temporolimbic Epilepsy and was asked by doctors to recall any unusual experiences. That's when he mentioned the incident during their camping trip. (00:13:09)

The doctor suggested he try contacting a UFO researcher about what had happened, and so that's what he did. He contacted Ray Fowler who in turn contacted Anthony Constantino, a professional hypnotist and English teacher. All four men agreed to be hypnotized separately to find out what really happened during their lost time. Each of them had a unique but similar account. They were all brought inside a sterile, dimly lit room inside the craft while big eyed creatures with long fingers probed and analyzed nearly every part of their bodies. (00:13:42)

The creatures that examined them had four fingers in each hand and metallic looking large eyes. Even though all four were artists, for some reason they could not put a proper image of the creatures together. Aside from that, Jack was able to see his friends and brother being examined as they were made to sit on a plastic like bench, but were unable to resist or do anything to protest what was happening. (00:14:05)

Various skin samples and fluids were taken which included humiliating experiments since the creatures wanted to see their physiological and anatomical makeup. Today, the Allagash Abduction remains to be one of the most popular accounts of alien abduction ever made. After the long hypnosis sessions with the men, Constantino was asked if he believed what they were saying was true and he responded, I do. After working with those guys, I was scared. I still am.
