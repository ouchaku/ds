The Salter Encounter 4-5分

ほとんどの場合、UFOアブダクションは悲惨で屈辱的な体験である。しかし、場合によっては、アブダクションのプロセスが実りあるものになることもある。

ジョン・サルター・ジュニアと彼の息子ジョン3世がそうだった。ノースダコタ大学の教員であったジョン・ジュニアは、南部諸州での講演ツアーを予定していた。

1988年3月、彼が息子に連れられてピックアップトラックで国道61号線を走り、最初の講演に向かったときのことである。その1時間後、ふたりは逆方向に走っていることに気がついた。二人は一晩休んで、翌朝旅を続けることにした。

二人は前の晩に何が起こったのか理解できずに途方に暮れた。突然、2人は銀色に輝く 「エネルギー・フィールド 」を持つUFOを見て衝撃を受けた。

その物体は、彼らが走っていた道路の上空に 「出現 」したのだ。ジョン・ジュニアと彼の息子は、自分たちが見ているものに「見覚えがある」感覚を覚え、フラッシュバックを起こし始めた。

二人は顔を見合わせ、前日、自分たちがいま夢中になっている物体に足を止められたことを思い出した。

エイリアンの一団がピックアップトラックに向かってきたとき、二人は車から降りた。

最初、ジョン・ジュニアは子供たちの集団かと思ったが、半分人間で半分エイリアンのような背の高い存在を見た。その奇妙な生き物の集団は、二人をその物体の方向へ連れ去った。

二人は怯えながらも、異星人は自分たちを傷つけないと感じた。ジョン・ジュニアは後に、奇妙な存在に守られている感覚を覚えたと語っている。船に向かう途中、彼はつまずいたが、一種の「エネルギー」のおかげで転んだり怪我をすることはなかった。

ジョン・ジュニアと彼の息子は二人とも湾曲した壁のある部屋に案内され、歯医者で使われる折りたたみ式の椅子に座らされた。

椅子に横たわり、検査を受ける間、二人とも「固定されている」ような感覚を覚えたと後に語っている。

ジョン・ジュニアの検査は、鼻の穴にインプラントが挿入されるところから始まった。次に首の横に器具のようなものが注射され、胸の上にも注射された。

下垂体、甲状腺、胸腺という3つの腺に関係する場所である。下垂体、甲状腺、胸腺の3つの腺が人間の成長、代謝、免疫をコントロールしていることをジョンは知っていた。

テスト終了後、ジョン・ジュニアは奇妙な異星人との 「絆 」を感じた。彼はまた、再会するというメッセージも感じた。この奇妙な状況は、ジョンと息子が家に戻った後、さらに奇妙な展開を見せることになる。

ジョン・ジュニアは健康状態が徐々に良くなっていることに気づいた。指の爪と髪の毛は早く太くなり、額の傷跡は薄くなり始め、ついにはほとんど消えてしまった。

ジョン・ジュニアが語ったもうひとつの驚くべき事実は、彼は喫煙者で、禁煙したいという願望と闘っていたが、今では考えもせずに禁煙できるようになったということだった。ソルター夫妻にとって、この誘拐事件はハッピーエンドのようだった。

ジョン・ジュニアは立派な職業人として知られており、彼を知る人々は、それが驚くべきことであるとしても、彼の話を否定することはできなかった。

ソルター夫妻の事件は、1988年のCBS映画『未知からの訪問者』でドラマ化された。

The Salter Encounter 4–5 minutes

In most cases, a UFO abduction is a harrowing, humiliating experience... one that the participant would love to forget. In some cases, however, the abduction process becomes a rewarding one.

Such was the case of John Salter Jr. and his son John III. John Jr. a faculty member of the University of North Dakota, was scheduled for a speaking tour of the Southern states.

It was in March 1988 when he, accompanied by his son, drove his pickup truck down Route 61 heading for his first engagement. For some unknown reason, John drove his truck off of his scheduled route, and one hour later, the two found themselves traveling in the opposite direction. They decided to take a rest for the night, and continue their trip the next morning.

The two men were at a loss to understand what had happened the evening before, though they discussed it at length as they continued down their original mapped-out drive. Suddenly the two were shocked to see a shining UFO with a silvery "energy field."

The object had just "appeared" over the road they were on. John Jr. and his son both had a "familiar" feeling about what they were seeing, and they began to have flashbacks, which refreshed their minds of the events of the previous day.

They looked at each other, both now aware that the day before they had been stopped dead in their tracks by the object they were now enthralled with.

Both men had stepped out of the vehicle as a group of aliens came toward their pickup truck.

At first John Jr. thought he was seeing a group of children, until he saw a taller being, which seemed half-human, half-alien. The group of strange beings led the two men away in the direction of the object.

The two men, though frightened in one way, felt that the alien beings would not hurt them. John Jr. would later relate that he felt a sense of being protected by the strange entities. He had taken a stumble while they were making their way to the ship, but a type of "energy" kept him from falling and being hurt.

John Jr. and his son were both led into a room with curved walls, where they were placed in chairs which resembled the fold-back type used by dentists.

Both men would later recall a feeling of being "immobilized" while they laid back in the chairs, and were examined.

John Jr.'s examination began with an implant being inserted into his nostril, remarkably though, without pain. Next an instrument of some kind was injected into the side of his neck, and another one at the top of his chest.

He felt the three areas chosen by the beings had a medical significance: the placement related to three glands; the pituitary, the thyroid, and the thymus. John had enough medical knowledge to know the three glands regulated human growth, metabolism, and immunity.

After the completion of the tests, John Jr. felt a strange sense of "bonding" with the strange, alien beings. He also sensed a message which stated they would meet again. These strange circumstances would take even a more bizarre turn after John and his son had returned home.

John Jr. noticed a gradual improvement in his overall health. His fingernails and hair grew faster and thicker, and a scar on his forehead began to fade, and eventually almost disappeared.

Another amazing fact John Jr. related was that though he was a smoker who struggled with the desire to quit, he now was able to quit without even thinking about it. To both of the Salter men, the abduction seemed to have a happy ending.

John Jr. was known as a respectable, professional man, and those who knew him were hard pressed to discount his story, as remarkable as it may seem.

The case of the Salters was dramatized on the 1988 CBS movie, "Visitors From The Unknown."
